WiFi Sensing

WiFi is a wireless networking technology basically used for communication between devices. Apart from its primary use for communication , WiFi can be used as various sensing tasks. WiFi sensing is a new technology that reuses the existing WiFi infrastructure. In WiFi sensing, WiFi signals are used for several sensing assignments, such as health monitoring, environment monitoring, Home monitoring, elderly people monitoring, activity classification, gesture recognition, human identification, localization, human counting, WiFi imaging, fall detection and many more.
